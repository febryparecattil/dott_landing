Our vision

Healthcare beyond boundaries. Most families get expert care only when they can reach a hospital - we're building the reverse: hospital-quality care that reaches them, at home, anywhere. Because location should never decide the standard of care a family gets.

Mission

DOTT follows your family's entire health journey - from catching a risk early, to a specialist's second opinion, to treatment delivered at home, to staying well afterward. Doctors, nurses, diagnostics, and AI all work from one platform, so care stays continuous instead of starting over at every visit. It's expert medicine, led by real doctors, without the hospital in between.
